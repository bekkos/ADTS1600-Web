The Smart Mask

Smart mask will come with a speaker on the right side and a microphone on the left side. There will be a filter on the inside where you can take it off and on, to meet requirments in hygiene and sanitation. After the covid-19 situation, we have seen the use of masks and the problems that come from the use of masks. Our solution: The smart mask gives the user an easy way to communicate with others while also minimizing your chance of getting infected. The product is a simple-looking mask, with technological aspects that will change the mask-game. Our main focus and desired outcome for the product we want to develop is that the mask is user-friendly for everyone. Our top priority is safety for our users and reusability are key for our product to succeed, therefore we will strive to ensure that our product fulfil these requirements and such is our desirable outcome. Today’s masks are suitable for a small part of the population. By customizing it, more people will feel comfortable using one.

2020 has been marked by a pandemic. This pandemic has shown us that the need of good, safe and easy to use facemasks is higher than ever before. As a society, we were caught off-guard and the pandemic shone light on the many problems with today’s face-mask production and their lacking quality and ease of use. Our flagship product works to solve this issue by allowing the user a much more comfortable and safe product which will allow for fluent communication and much less risk of infection due to not having to remove the mask and/or touch your face. We will do this by giving the mask a speaker and a mic inside so that you can easily communicate with one another. We also want to make it easy hygienically to be able to change the inside of the mask without changing the whole mask. This is done by changing only the innermost layers of the mask and keeping the outside of the mask. We want to make the mask more suitable for everyday life, while still keeping it hygienic and minimize spreading the virus. We want to make the mask meet the users need for them to be willing to wear it, by making it more wearable yet still comply the requirements of the government.

We have developed an additional product for our Smart Mask. This app is suitable for all iOS users and Android users. The app helps your user-experience to be even greater, by making everything easier for the user. By having access to your phone, you can adjust the microphone and speaker volume. This is great for checking the mask’s battery level as well.

The Smart Link app is specially designed to match the mask and make the user feel more safe whilst using the product, since they get the opportunity to adjust the features of the mask without having to touch the front of the mask.
