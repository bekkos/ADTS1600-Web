The Smart Mask

After the covid-19 situation, we have seen the use of masks and the problems that come from the use of masks. Our solution: The smart mask gives the user an easy way to communicate with others while also minimizing your chance of getting infected. The product is a simple-looking mask, with technological aspects that will change the mask-game. Our main focus and desired outcome for the product we want to develop is that the mask is user-friendly for everyone. Our top priority is safety for our users and reusability are key for our product to succeed, therefore we will strive to ensure that our product fulfil these requirements and such is our desirable outcome.
